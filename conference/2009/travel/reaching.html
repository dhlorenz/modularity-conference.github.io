How to Reach AOSD.09

By Air

Several major airlines have connections to the Charlottesville Airport (CHO). Ground transportation is available through taxis or rental cars. The airport is a 25 minute drive (10 miles) from the proposed venue and hotels. The airport has 60 daily non-stop flights to/from:

  • Atlanta (Georgia)
  • Cincinnati (Ohio)
  • Detroit (Michigan)
  • Charlotte (North Carolina)
  • New York/LaGuardia
  • Philadelphia (Pennsylvania)
  • Washington/Dulles

CHO is served by:

  • Delta Connection
  • Northwest Airlines
  • United Express
  • US Airways Express

By Car

Directions to the University of Virginia from I-64 or I-66 (via US Route 29 North). At the University of Virginia, AOSD.09 will be held at Newcomb Hall (Building No. 30 in the U.Va. Central Grounds Map)
