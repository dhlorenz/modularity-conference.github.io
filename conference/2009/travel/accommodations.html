Accommodations

We recommend the following hotels in order of a weighted combination of quality, cost, and co-locality: Courtyard by Marriott, Hampton Inn, Red Roof Inn. For those looking for economy and a minimal distance walk to the conference venue, we recommend the Cavalier Inn. We recommend the Residence Inn as a last resort, due to its distance to the conference site. The Omni hotel on the downtown pedestrian mall is very nice, with regular city bus service to the conference site. For people wanting luxurious accommodations, we recommend the Boar's Head Inn, but be advised that you will need to drive to and pay for parking each date at the conference site.

The local accommodations below are listed based on the distance from the conference site at Newcomb Hall at the University of Virginia (nearest to farthest).
