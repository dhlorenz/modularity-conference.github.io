Registration

Register here for Modularity'14

"Early registration" fees apply for all registrations received before or on March 14, 2014 (anywhere in the world).

Starting from March 15, 2014 "Late registration" fees apply until April 21, 2014 included, then starting from April 22, 2014 "On site registration" fees will apply.

Registration fees for Modularity'14 are in Swiss Francs (CHF) and have to be paid in Swiss Francs.

Registration fees
  Regular Student *

Early (until 14 March)

 750 CHF 450 CHF

Late (from 15 March to 21 April)

 850 CHF 550 CHF

On site (from 22 April)

 950 CHF 650 CHF

Registration includes the FOAL workshop, social events, lunches and coffee breaks.

Cancellations are possible until April 11 and are subject to a 100 CHF processing fee. No refunds will be processed after this date.


(*) Student registrants are required to show their valid student ID card at the conference registration desk in Lugano. People who registered as students but do not show their valid student ID card will have to pay the regular on-site registration fee (CHF 950).
