Conference Location and Hotel

Sheraton Vancouver Wall Centre

Floor plan of the Wall Centre (pdf 2.5Mb)

Sheraton Vancouver Wall Centre Hotel
1088 Burrard Street Vancouver
British Columbia V6Z 2R9
Phone (604) 331-1000

A special rate of $179 per night is available at the Sheraton Wall Centre. Additionally, there will be a one time hotel rebate of $100 for regular attendees and $50 for student attendees per room for staying at the conference hotel. By staying at the conference hotel you help AOSD qualify for discounts on room rental and other charges. The rebate provides a way to pass some of those savings back to you. The hotel rebate will be applied to your credit card after we have confirmed your registration at the Sheraton Wall Centre.

The Sheraton Wall Centre is located in the heart of Downtown Vancouver, minutes away from Stanley Park (one of the world's largest urban parks), Robson Street (shopping district), and Yaletown (nightlife, restaurants, and bars). Vancouver is a great walking city. The links provided are some suggestions of walks that you might want to do in the area. There are more here.
