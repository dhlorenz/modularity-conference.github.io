Policy
Authors
At least one author of each accepted paper must register to ensure that the paper is included in the conference program and the proceedings. The deadline for author registration is February, 1st 2010. Papers that do not have an associated registered author by this date will not be included in the proceedings.
Student status
Students are defined as full-time students, enrolled for degrees or diplomas with a university or another similar institution.
Cancellation
All cancellations must be made by writing to the AOSD.10 Secretariat. Cancellations received before March 1st, 2010 are fully refundable minus 50 € processing and administration charge. Cancellations received after March 1st are non-refundable. No refunds will be given for non-attendance. Telephone cancellations are not accepted.
After registration
If properly processed, your successful registration will be acknowledged by email, which you may use as a receipt of payment. A formal invoice will also be issued to you in hardcopy at the time of the conference.

Feel free to contact congres@pgl-congres.com for any question regarding the conference registration.
