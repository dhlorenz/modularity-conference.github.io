The AOSD 2003 has selected the Sheraton Boston as the conference hotel. It is important that conference attendees book rooms at the Sheraton for the conference to fill our quota for using the meeting rooms for free. We ask conference attendees to stay at the Sheraton if possible.  The discounted prices for AOSD 2003 are, per night:
  - $149 (single)
  - $169 (double)

The instructions for online reservation are:

Go to: http://www.starwood.com/sheraton/meetings/attend_enter_code.html
  - Enter Boston, Massachusetts
  - Your Meeting Code is 11579
  - Select The Sheraton Boston Hotel
  - Enter your Arrival and Departure Dates, Select Continue
  - Confirmation appears at the end and we also offer an email confirmation

In addition to booking their rooms, you can view a slide show of the hotel and general hotel information.  You can sign up for our award winning frequent guest program, Starwood Preferred Guest on the web site.

For reservations questions, attendees should email boston.sales@sheraton.com  or call 1-800-325-3535.
