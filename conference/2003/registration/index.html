The conference registration website is available at https://www.cteusa.com/aosd1

The AOSD 2003 has selected the Sheraton Boston as the conference hotel. It is important that conference attendees book rooms at the Sheraton for the conference to fill our quota for using  the meeting rooms for free. We ask conference attendees to stay at the Sheraton if possible.

The discounted prices for AOSD 2003 are, per night:
- $149 (single)
- $169 (double) (for two persons)

Early registration costs $ 450 (regular) and $ 150 (student). There are nine half-day tutorials, each for $ 160 (regular and student) and a package of four is $ 480 (4 for 3).

For more information refer to the Lodging Page.  For questions about registration, please contact aosd-local@ccs.neu.edu
