Royal Kings Arms Hotel

Cost: 58.50 GBP per night (including Full English Breakfast)

Partners sharing the room will be charged 10.00GBP per night. This includes full English breakfast. Please ensure you request accommodation for two people.

Hotel Location

Situated in the heart of Lancaster and opposite the famous castle, this hotel is convenient for the railway station and city centre. The University campus is about 3 miles away - taxi 5.00GBP and there are frequent local buses from the City Centre.
