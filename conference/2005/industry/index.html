TheServerSide Industry Track

AOSD.05 is featuring a full industry track with keynotes, invited talks, exhibitions, tutorials, and a panel with presentations by leading developers, practitioners, and authors of aspect technologies. There will also be BOFs, social events, and opportunities to interact with leaders and the community of developers and researchers working with aspect technology.
