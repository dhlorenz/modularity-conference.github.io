AOSD.05 is looking for student volunteers!

Requirements for Applicants

To apply, please make sure that you fulfill our requirements:

  • You are a graduate or PhD student in the field of computer science
  • You speak English fluently. (If you speak any other languages that is fine, but English is a must!)

Duties

Your duties as a student volunteer will (among others) be the following during the conference:

  • Presence from Sunday, March 13 to Saturday March 19
  • Workshops and tutorials: guarding doors and technical support
  • Technical program: guarding doors and microphones
  • Social events: guarding doors and overall help
  • Registration desk and information booth

Before and after the conference, you will help in:

  • Stuffing the delegates' packages
  • Arranging and clearing the conference area and workshop and tutorial rooms
  • Put on signs for information and messages

Benefits

Here is what you get in return for your effort:

  • Free admission to tutorials, workshops and the main conference during your spare time
  • Free lunches and coffee breaks on all conference days as well as welcome receptions on Monday and Wednesday
  • 2 AOSD’05 t-shirts
  • Certificate of participation in the organization of AOSD 2005

How to Apply

Your application must include the following:

  • A short CV (no more than one page)
  • A list of languages you speak
  • A recommendation letter from a faculty member of your university if you are not a student of Illinois Institute of Technology

Please send your application (in English) via e-mail to Shubhanan Bakre (bakrshuiit.edu)

Deadline for applications is January 10, 2005

Notification: January 30, 2005

Selection will be done on a first received, first accepted basis among suitable applications.

If you have any questions, feel free to send the SV chair, Shubhanan Bakre (bakrshuiit.edu)
