AOSD-Europe Sponsorships

AOSD.05 is providing up to 7 free registrations for European students that want to attend the conference. These free registrations are sponsored by AOSD-Europe: European Network of Excellence in AOSD.

Eligible for receiving such a free registration are European students only (where this is defined as a student in an institution of higher education in an EU member or an associated country). We especially encourage those European students that do not have strong support from their institutions and are nevertheless conducting research in AOSD on their own initiative. To apply, please send the following to the Conference Chair via email to mezini@informatik.tu-darmstadt.de:

  • a short CV including a list of publications, if any;
  • a short summary of no more than one page of your AOSD research interests;
  • a short explanation as why you want to attend AOSD.05, including e.g., a list of events you intend to attend, and why your travel cannot be supported by your home institution.
