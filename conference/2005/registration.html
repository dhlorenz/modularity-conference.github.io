Registration

Online registration is now closed. Onsite registration begins Sunday March 13, 6 pm to 8 pm in the Empire Ballroom (7th floor)

If you have any questions or comments about registration please send an email to registration@aosd.net.

Important Dates

Last day for late registration:March 7, 2005
(Registration will also be available at the conference.)

What's included

Regular Registration includes:

  • Attendance at the AOSD main conference (16-18 March 2005 inclusive)
  • Attendance at TheServerSide Industry Track (16-18 March 2005 inclusive)
  • Attendance at any workshop to which you have been accepted (14-15 March 2005)
  • Conference Pack (including 1 copy of the proceedings - extra copies can be ordered separately)
  • Ticket to Conference Reception
  • One Banquet Ticket

Student Registration includes:

  • Attendance at the AOSD main conference (16-18 March 2005 inclusive)
  • Attendance at TheServerSide Industry Track (16-18 March 2005 inclusive)
  • Attendance at any workshop to which you have been accepted (14-15 March 2005)
  • Conference Pack (including 1 copy of the proceedings – extra copies can be ordered separately)
  • Ticket to Conference Reception
  • NB. Banquet ticket NOT included. A ticket can be purchased separately

Note: In order to give the student's rate, we need proof of student status. The proof can be either a student id card, or a confirmation from the dean of the faculty, or from the university administration (confirmation of registration / enrolment), where you are officially registered as a regular student. In short, you should have a valid matriculation number with a university. You will be required to present the proof of your student status when you register at the conference registration desk. Please make sure that you have it, otherwise you will not be able to register as a student.

Accommodation

We have a special arrangement with the InterContinental Hotel. The rate for the AOSD.05 participants is $149 per room per night. Please use the online booking form to get the special rate. Note that this group rate is valid for booking only until 22-Feb-2005. The unused portion of the room block will be released to the hotel after the cut-off date. Reservation/name changes received after the cut-off date will be accepted at the group rate subject to availability.

Student Accommodation: If you would like to share a room with a another student please contact Shubhanan Bakre at bakrshuiit.edu. You will receive a list of other students who would like to share a room.
