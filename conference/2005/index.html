Aspect-oriented software development (AOSD) technologies enable the modularization of crosscutting concerns within software systems. AOSD techniques and tools, applied at all stages of the software lifecycle, are changing the way software is developed in various application domains, ranging from enterprise to embedded systems.

The annual AOSD international conference is the premier event on aspect-oriented technologies and practices, bringing together leading researchers and developers to discuss the latest developments in the field. Building on the three successful prior conferences, the program of AOSD.05 will include:

  • Keynotes by Grady Booch and Dave Thomas
  • A research paper track with 17 papers
  • Invited presentations by the developers of all the leading commercial AOP tools
  • A panel on current trends in industry adoption of AOSD
  • Tutorials on various topics and ranging from beginner to advanced
  • Reports describing practical experience with AOSD technologies
  • Workshops for in-depth discussion of advanced topics
  • Demonstrations of leading-edge technologies
  • Birds of a Feather sessions
  • A student event where students can discuss their work with leading experts

AOSD.05 will be held at the InterContinental hotel located in the heart of Chicago's Magnificent Mile. Complementing the technical program there will also be a range of social activities encouraging informal discussions among peers.
