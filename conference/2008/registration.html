Registration Information

Regular registration to the main conference includes:

  • Participation in the workshops on Monday 31 March and Tuesday 1 April (if workshops are attended, lunch is included)
  • participation in the conference from Wednesday 2 April till Friday 4 April included
  • welcome reception (Wednesday 2 April)
  • banquet (Thursday 3 April)
  • paper copy of the proceedings
  • lunch (3 days: Wednesday 2 April till Friday 4 April)
  • 2 coffee breaks per day
  • 10-ride public transportation ticket
  • participation in the evening social events on monday and tuesday

Student Registration to the main conference includes:

  • participation in the workshops on Monday 31 March and Tuesday 1 April (if workshops are attended, lunch is included)
  • participation in the conference from Wednesday 2 April till Friday 4 April included
  • access to the welcome reception (Wednesday 2 April)
  • lunch (3 days: Wednesday 2 April till Friday 4 April)
  • 2 coffee breaks per day
  • 10-ride public transportation ticket
  • participation in the evening social events on monday and tuesday

Single Day Registration to the main conference includes:

  • participation in the conference activities (workshop or technical/industry program - excluding tutorials) on a particular day
  • evening social event (except for the banquet for which a separate ticket needs to be purchased)
  • 2 coffee breaks on that day
  • lunch for that day
  • one-day public transportation ticket

Tutorial registration includes:

  • participation in the tutorial
  • tutorial notes and handouts
  • lunch and coffee breaks

Important note regarding participation in the workshop(s): Please note that individual workshops may have their own access regulations. Please check the corresponding webpages and contact the workshop organizers regarding participation if required.

Cancellation Notes:

Registration cancellation on or before Friday 21st of March, 2008: 90% refund.

Registration cancellation after 21st of March, 2008: no refund possible of registration fee.

One exception to this rule: people with a single day registration to attend a particular tutorial can cancel without any cancellation fee IF that particular tutorial is cancelled (this can happen in the case that the minimum number of participants for a tutorial is not reached)

Cancellation must be in writing (by letter, fax, or e-mail) to the Conference Registration office. The date of postmark will be used when considering a refund. All refunds will be made after the conference.
