AOSD.08 Postcard Lottery

AOSD.08 will have an extra exciting moment at its banquet when the winner of the very first AOSD lottery is drawn! If you receive(d) one of the AOSD.08 promotional postcards, don't throw it away as it will serve as your lottery ticket. Make sure your name is on the address field at the back of the card, and hand it in when you come pick up your conference bag at the local registration desk. The lucky winner will receive a free registration for AOSD.09!

The rules:

  • Only one card per registered participant of AOSD.08 will be accepted.
  • The winner receives a free registration for one of the next editions of the International Conference on Aspect-Oriented Software Development, either the 2009 or 2010 edition as per the winner's choice.
  • The free registration covers the base, non-student registration package of AOSD.09 or AOSD.10, plus base registration for the workshop days if not included, but not covering other additional costs such as tutorials and other items not included in the base package.
  • The free registration is offered on a personal basis to the winner and is non-transferrable, nor can it be paid out in monetary or other form.

We'd like to wish you good luck in participating in the lottery. And why not display the card prominently on your desk? It'll give us some visibility, and you won't forget to take it with you when you're leaving for Brussels!
