Student Extravaganza

Sponsored by

As with previous editions, the AOSD.08 Student Extravaganza consists of several events and efforts to entice and support students doing research in AOSD to come to the conference. The main events, the Spring School and the Poster Event, will take place on the Sunday preceding the conference and on Tuesday night during the reception to maximize visibility. The Student Wiki serves as a virtual meeting place before the conference and provides updates on social events for students.
