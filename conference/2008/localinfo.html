Local Information

Want to know how to get to AOSD2008 from the hotels? Download the reaching AOSD08 from the hotels handout. Also, take a look at the AOSD VUB map, and the map of the building AOSD.08 is in.

To help you navigate Brussels we have provided a map.

The 7th International conference on Aspect-Oriented Software Development will be held at the campus of the Vrije Universiteit Brussel in Brussels, the capital of Belgium and the European Union.

The conference is held at the Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB), on its campus 'Oefenplein' Etterbeek. The Campus is located in the southeast of the city centre, about 15 minutes by subway. Please note that this campus houses both the Dutch-speaking Vrije Universiteit Brussel (where AOSD 2008 is taking place) and the French-speaking Universit√© Libre de Bruxelles (ULB).

There are several conference hotels (all located in the city center of Brussels) with special rates for the AOSD conference. More information can be found under the accommodation link. The distance between the conference hotels and the university is about 25 minutes (by subway). Every registered participant will get tickets for using the Brussels public transportation system in their conference bag.
