Birds of a Feather ... Flock together!

AOSD.08 aims to promote discussion to the greatest extent possible. To that end, like last year, we provide rooms and equipment for organised as well as impromptu “Birds of a Feather“ (BoF) Sessions. A BoF session provides an informal forum for discussions related to the AOSD community and its topics. Attendees who want to get together with others to share their interests, goals, technologies, environments, or backgrounds are invited to organise and attend a Birds of a Feather Session.

BoF Purposes

BoFs can be used to continue a discussion that began in a workshop, to allow more detailed follow-up discussion on a paper or demo, for a group to get together to pursue a common interest, to discuss research directions, or to discuss contrary opinions.

Registration

For the better advertisement of and to allocate space for your BoFs, please send an email to bofs at aosd.net. Please include:

  • the title of your BoF
  • a short description of the goals of your BoF session
  • particular equipment you may need (e.g., projector)
  • your name, affiliation, and preferred e-mail address
  • approximate size of the audience you expect (so we can reserve an appropriate sized room)

Times

Rooms and time slots will be announced later upon the finalisation of the conference schedule.

Organisation

BoFs that are requested before the start of the conference will be announced on the conference webpage, including a short description of the discussed topic. All BoFs will be announced during the conference in all tracks. Additionally, posters will advertise BoF events with all necessary information such as topic, room, time etc. Sign-up sheets will be placed near the registration desk, where organisers can reserve rooms on a first-come first-served basis during the conference, but BoF attendees are asked to sign-up, too.

Questions or suggestions? Please feel free to email bofs at aosd.net.

We are looking forward to seeing you in Brussels !
