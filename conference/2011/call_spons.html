Call for Sponsors

The International Conference on Aspect-Oriented Software Development (AOSD) is the premier conference on software modularity, with an emphasis on modular structures that cut across traditional abstraction boundaries. The conference offers several tracks, including the Research and Industry Tracks, which bring together leading researchers and practitioners working in such fields as software engineering, programming languages, systems, and others.

AOSD attracts an international audience of 200 participants from different communities and mixing practitioners and academics. AOSD 2011 seeks to foster richer notions of modularity and abstractions for software and software-intensive systems increasing the emphasis on modularity not only of code, but across lifecycle artifacts (e.g., from requirements to tests). AOSD 2011 is concerned with modularity across software ecosystems, including the organizations and processes that define, develop, deploy, operate, protect, evolve and use these systems in a more agile way. AOSD’11 will dedicate a whole day to the industrial practices related to an increased modularity in software development. This day will include an industrial keynote, tutorials, tool demonstrations and industrial presentations.

The Venue. In 2011, the AOSD conference will be held in Porto de Galinhas, Brazil. Porto de Galinhas has been elected by a number of tourist guides one of the best beaches in Brazil. It is well-known for its natural pools, consequence of its coral riffs, and has a rich tourist infrastructure. Porto de Galinhas is located 50Km to the south of Recife. Recife is one of the largest cities in Brazil and a hub for technology-related companies. In particular, the old city port went through a restoration 15 years ago and is now known as the Digital Port ("Porto Digital") due to the large concentration of IT companies that it hosts.

We are looking for both academic and industrial sponsors for the organization of this conference. By sponsoring this event you have the opportunity to advertise your tools, methodologies and success stories and to make the conference audience aware of them.


Why Sponsor AOSD?

AOSD 2011 is the main international event where academy and industry professionals share experiences and learn about the latest developments related to the development of modular software. In addition, it is an already established conference, since this is its 10th edition and also has an enviable impact factor.

The World Wide Impact of AOSD. For example, in Microsoft's Academic Search, AOSD ranks among the 15 most important conferences in the areas of Software Engineering and Programming Languages, with an average of 17.6 citations per paper, higher than traditional conferences such as ICSE and SIGSOFT FSE. Moreover, the conference has already been classified as an international first class event in the latest Qualis/CAPES ranking. The latter is used by Brazilian research agencies to evaluate the quality of scientific events and journals. This is a reference event for all Software Engineering (Computer Science and related areas) researchers in Brazil and worldwide. Thus, supporting AOSD is an excellent investment for your organization.

A Unique Opportunity for Visibility and Partnerships. In fact, your support for AOSD conference is a unique opportunity to expose the name of your organization for a large community of students, researchers and professionals in the software industry. In this edition, the conference is expected to count with more than 200 participants from the academia and the industry, not to mention that the website of the conference is and will be visited by hundreds of people every week, until the day of the event. Your contribution will be of extreme importance to cover the many expenses that are not covered by the budget of the event, and for the success of the conference as a whole.

Sponsors are very important to the conference, and the conference committee ensures that their contribution be well recognized. We list the proposed levels of sponsorships and their corresponding benefits in the following section. Corporate support can also be targeted to a particular event or activity during AOSD 2011. For example, you might fund the coffee breaks, opening cocktail, conference dinner or internet room. We also welcome suggestions of others benefits that would make your sponsorship more attractive. Supporting AOSD 2011 would be a great opportunity for your company to initiate or consolidate a partnership with the academic and practitioners’ AOSD communities. Furthermore, AOSD 2011 is also an excellent recruiting venue for companies and universities looking for highly qualified graduate students.

Bringing Your Organization and Leading Researchers Together. The technical program of the 10th edition of the AOSD conference will bring together the contributions of many widely-recognized researchers in the areas of Software Engineering and Programming Language. Levels of Sponsorship and Benefits

AOSD 2011 welcomes four levels of general sponsorship: Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Bronze. In addition, we welcome support on specific activities and resources.

Platinum Sponsor - USD 4,900
The Platinum sponsor will get the highest level of visibility before and during the conference and the widest range of benefits. In fact, several benefits are exclusive to the Platinum sponsor, such as those in the four first bullets in the following. In addition, your organization will get the highest visibility in all the announcements and publicity material. The benefits of a Platinum Sponsor are:

  • Inclusion of logo on event T-shirt (free distribution to all participants of the conference)

  • You will get 20% discount on all registrations of company employees

  • Banners of your company (provided by the company) visible in strategic locations at the conference site
  • We will announce your company's contribution and offer a special acknowledgment at the opening and closing sessions of the conference, as the Platinum Sponsor.
  • Your company name, logo and hyperlink will be included in the conference web pages, in a highlighted location, maintained at http://www.aosd.net/2011.
  • You will have the opportunity to include material and small give-aways with the conference material for each attendee.
  • Your company name and logo in organizer’s and volunteers' T-shirt
  • Your company name and logo in all attendees’ bags, in the conference proceedings, and in the official conference program booklets.
  • You will get free attendance (for two people) at the dinner event
  • You will have a booth (6m2) in the exhibit space to expose and promote your products, or even recruit students and professionals, and highest priority in choice of site in exhibition hall.
  • You will get the opportunity to organize a BoF or present a tool
