Accomodations in Bonn

  • Recommended Hotels
  • Other Nearby Hotels
  • Map and Search Faciliy

Hotel prices in Germany always include service and tax, and typically include breakfast. About 2/3 of the hotels in Bonn offer a single room between 55€ and 70€ per night. In the city center, where the conference venue is located, prices are between 70€ and 120€. The city center is reachable by public transport within at most 15 minutes from most parts of the city.

Student Accommodation: If you would like to share a room with another student please post to the AOSD.net Wiki.

The unused portion of the room block will be released to the hotel after the cut-off date. Reservations or change requests received after the cut-off date will be accepted at the group rate subject to availability.
