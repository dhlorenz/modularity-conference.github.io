Weather

After a comparatively cold winter, we hope that spring will finally come to Bonn during the conference week. Expected temperatures are between -3 and +13°C (around 27 - 55°F). Also prepare yourself for rain or—outside of downtown Bonn—even snow.

For a detailed local forecast, refer to the web pages of Deutsche Welle, Germany's international broadcasting corporation.
