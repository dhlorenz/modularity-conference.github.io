<p>The Bonn Museum of Modern Art built by Axel Schultes is one of the most important postwar museum constructions in Germany, making it the culmination of the museum boom. The collection focuses on German art after 1945.</p>
<p>This museum is located along the so-called &quot;museum mile&quot;, and if you choose to visit it, this is the perfect time to jump into another museum, too!</p>
