University main building

University of Bonn
Regina-Pacis-Weg 3
D-53113 Bonn


or Local Map

The main conference venue is a baroque era's Electoral Palace, which today houses the university. It is located in the heart of Bonn, just 400m walking distance from main station and 450m to the Rhine promenade. Hotels of various categories are within 5-10 minutes walking distance.

Floor plan (Monday/Tuesday)

The conference area is located on the first floor of the university main building. Note: American visitors would call this floor the second floor!
