Student Accommodations

Accommodations for volunteers and grant holders

All rooms available at the Hotel Löhndorf, Stockenstr. 6, 53113 Bonn have been reserved for student volunteers and holders of student grants. Successful applicants are asked to book as soon as possible. Rooms not booked before February 22, 2006 will be made available to other conference participants. Students can book the rooms for March 17–25, 2006.

There are several options available:

  • Single rooms with TV/phone/sink/bathrobe (shower/toilet shared by 2 single rooms): 40€ per pesrson per night
  • Single rooms with TV/phone/shower/toilet inside the rooms: 65€ per person per night
  • Double rooms with TV/phone/shower/toilet: 42€ per person per night (double occupancy only)

Booking must be done with complete adress etc. untill February 22, 2006 per mail (info@hotel-loehndorf-bonn.de) or fax (+49 228 69 57 12). Accepted student volunteers and grant holders will be informed about the keyword they should quote in order to get the special prices mentioned above.

Prices include service, tax and continental breakfast. All rooms are non-smoking.
