Conference Banquet

Thursday 25 March 2004
Imperial Hotel, Blackpool

The Imperial Hotel is a traditional English Hotel and one of Blackpool's most famous. It is a comfortable, impressive venue situated prominently on North Promenade.

Built in the late 1800s, the beautiful Victorian hotel has recently been restored to its former glory. One of the Imperial's earliest guests, Charles Dickens, described the hotel as 'charming'.

Further Details:

17.30:Depart from Lancaster University campus at 17:30 and travel by coach to the Imperial (45-60 minutes). Transportation is included in the Banquet ticket
18.30:Arrive at the Imperial Hotel and enjoy pre-dinner drinks in the grand Washington Room
19.00:A 3-course Lancashire Dinner will be served in the Lancastrian with live entertainment
23.15:Depart (coaches will drop off in Lancaster University Campus and then Lancaster City Centre).
