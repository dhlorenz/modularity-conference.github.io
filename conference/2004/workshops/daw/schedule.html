Dynamic Aspects Workshop (DAW04)

Schedule

The workshop takes place on Tuesday, March 23rd, in room LT3 of the George Fox Building at Lancaster University (see the campus map).

All presented papers are published in the DAW'04 Proceedings, published as RIACS Technical Report 04.01.

Please note that the schedule may still be subject to change.
