Dynamic Aspects Workshop (DAW04)

Posters

The poster exhibition starts during the morning coffee break (10:30). It is open to all conference participants during lunch and coffee breaks. Poster viewers will be given the opportunity to talk to the authors of posters. Posters will be on display until the workshop closes.

The following posters will be exhibited during the workshop:

  • Sufyan Almajali, Tzilla Elrad
    A Dynamic Aspect Oriented C++ Using MOP with Minimal Hook Weaving Approach
    Published in the DAW'04 Proceedings.

  • Christoph Bockisch, Michael Haupt, Mira Mezini, Klaus Ostermann
    Steamloom: A JVM Supporting Dynamic Join Points
    Organiser poster, published in AOSD'04.

  • Peter Ebraert, Eric Tanter
    A Concern-Based Approach to Dynamic Software Evolution
    Published in the DAW'04 Proceedings.

  • Stephan Herrmann, Christine Hundt, Katharina Mehner
    Dynamism in Object Teams
    Organiser poster, abstract.

  • Alexandre Vasseur
    Java Dynamic AOP and Runtime Weaving - How does AspectWerkz address it?
    Published in the DAW'04 Proceedings.

  • Yang Yu, Jörg Kienzle
    Towards an Efficient Aspect Precedence Model
    Published in the DAW'04 Proceedings.
