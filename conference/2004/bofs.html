Birds of a Feather Sessions (BOF)

Birds of a Feather Sessions provide an informal forum for discussions related to a particular community or topic. No registration is required for BOFs. Rooms will be made available for BOF sessions on Monday and Tuesday evening. A sign-up sheet will be placed near the registration desk, where attendees can reserve rooms on a first-come first-served basis. Please email registration@aosd.net if you expect a large audience.
